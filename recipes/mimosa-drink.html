Mimosa

Description

Mimosas are a delicious combination of sparkling wine, and orange juice.
They are simple, fun, and perfect to serve to company.

As with many popular cocktails, the origin story of the Mimosa is somewhat debated.
However, most credit Frank Meier, a bartender at the Ritz Hotel in Paris, with creating the cocktail in 1925.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • ¾ cup champagne, chilled

Steps

  1. Divide orange juice evenly into two flute glasses; top with chilled Champagne and serve.
